Fox HR Application

Fox HR is next-gen human resource management product especially designed for intelligent start-ups, small and medium scale companies under any scale and business vertical for the betterment of workforce management irrespective of geographical boundaries covering the entire spectrum from Onboarding to Off boarding of an employee and beyond. Fox HR being a SaaS application is hosted on AWS, and the clients of Fox HR have the option of plug & play. i.e., the client signs the license and start using the system as plug N use. Fox HR caters to all the needs of a companyâ€™s human resource department, its employees and management monitoring requirements. Fox HR is an integrated software application that combines many human resources functions, including benefits administration, payroll processing, attendance management, leave management and much more. Fox HR have both Web & Mobile (iOS & Android) App for their use. The Mobile App have the Employee Self Service (ESS) features such as options to view details of the employee information, raise various Employee Requests and take actions on them. Currently, Fox HR interface has 2 languages, - English & Arabic.

Fox HR, being a Human Resource Management System (HRMS) have the following functionality.

  • Employee Management
  • Attendance Management
  • Leave Management
  • Payroll Management
  • Request Management
  • Notification Management
  • Workflow and Tasks Management
  • Overview / Dashboards
  • Analytical Reports
  • Client Configuration
